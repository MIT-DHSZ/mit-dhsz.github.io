Notability Tutorial

A powerful yet simple note-taking app on iOS system

Tutorial Introduction

23/11/19 10:55

Notability is a powerful yet simple note-taking app on iOS system. Users can use this app to do note-taking and PDF annotation. With an Apple Pencil, you can even have better note-taking experiences than that of using conventional notebooks and pens, and your study efficiency can be hugely improved. If you are struggling with how to use Notability, read ahead and your problem will be solved.



In this tutorial, we will teach you:




  • How to take notes using Notability

  • How to import and share your notes

  • Make use of the auto backup

  • Other useful functions



If you have a iPad and want to save your energy from carrying a pile of notebooks everyday, I highly recommend this app to you.
