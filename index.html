Master In Tech

Boost your Productivity via Technology

Why do we exist?

As people’s reliance on technological devices boosts recent years, how to effectively use these devices to maximum the work efficiency has become a big problem for everyone.

Who are we?

Master in Tech is a non-profit project aiming to help you build relationships with nowadays technological world. We offer tips for better use of your computers and iPads. On this website, you can find tutorials of efficiency-enhancing softwares such as Microsoft Teams, Notion and Microsoft OneNote.

What else do we offer?

You can also find recommendations to some useful softwares that you have never heard of. We have all kinds of softwares on different systems, so regardless of the system you are using, this website will be helpful to you. Our email address is at the bottom of the page. You can contact us if you have any problems.

“It is only when they go wrong that machines remind you how powerful they are.” -- Clive James, Broadcaster and Journalist.

MIT Survey

To better help the students, we have made a form for you to fill. The form will be asking questions like your technology usages and specific technology-related knowledges you would like to learn. It would only take maximum 2 minutes to complete and we really hope you can do us a favor to fill it.
